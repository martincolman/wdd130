    Washington

    The state of Washington is beautiful. It is surrounded by many lakes and trees. Here there is a big-name city called Seattle. Seattle is a place where you can see and experience many things. There is a market called Pike Place Market, here you can see fisherman throwing fish back and forth, or you can shop at the many small stores within Pike Place. Aside from that you are able to go to the landmark they call the Space Needle, this needle is beautiful, and towers the sky at a height of 605 feet. This is a beautiful place and I would recommend it to anyone to visit.
